ABOUT-US

Greetings.

Institute of Education (IE) has been established at Parbatipur Upazilla under Dinajpur District in it's own campus beside Parbatipur bus terminal. The IE will be constituted by brilliant teachers with excellent academic result. There will be no teacher below Masters level with having 2nd class in all exam from SSC to Masters.. Teachers and support staff will be trained regularly to cope with updated information. Principal of the IE will be a retired college Principal or having at least 10 years experienced teacher. Primarily, IE will start working with KG to class Five. Initially, each class will consists of 20 students. In each class 2 students will get full tuition waive based on merit and economic status. The IE campus and all class room will be under CCTV coverage for monitoring and security reason. Academic session will start from 01 January, 2023. Academic activities will be based on national curriculum. Beside this, learning of the holy Quran along with moral education is included. Special emphasis will be given on English, Math and General knowledge for the enrichment of students.
